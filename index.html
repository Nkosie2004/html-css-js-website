    About SSSV Choir

    The Summerstrand student village Choir is a vibrant community ensemble dedicated to sharing the joy of music with people of all ages and backgrounds. Founded in 2024. Our mission is to promote the power of music in bringing people together. Under the direction of conductor Sbahle Zondi, a passionate conductor. The choir has performed at numerous events, fundraising event. We are proud to be a part of our community’s cultural fabric and work to ensure that our performances are accessible and meaningful to everyone. Whether you’re an experienced singer or someone who just loves to sing, we invite you to join us for rehearsals. No audition is required — just bring your love for music and a desire to share it with others.
